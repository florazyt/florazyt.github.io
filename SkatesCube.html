SkatesCube

Type:
Personal UI/UX Project

Duration:
10-12.2020

SkatesCube is a ice skates rental system designed for ice skating club to upgrade their skates management and for customers to enjoy better skating experience. The design includes ticketing mobile app, skates locker product, and  ice-skating club interior concept.

Specification

User flow, Information architecture, Wireframe, Interactive prototype, User Testing

Tool

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Rhino, Keyshot.

Background

Skating is an artistic sport that combines speed, music, dance, and skills. Because of its fun, skating is often an excellent choice for me to gather with relatives and friends in my free time.

Winter Olympics raise people’s attentions.

International skating idols attract many fans.

More children learn skating as interest.

Customer Survey

86 Samples from Internet and face-to-face reasearch at skating club

User Journey

Problem

Customers are generally unsatisfied with skates and protector renting services of skating club. Current manual skates renting system cannot assure the tidiness of the club.

Some beginner and one-time experiencer prefer courses, while the information at entrance are so complicated that they have to keep on asking staffs, causing unnecessary.

People’s bad experience with skates and services will directly leave them ill impression of the club. Club have to keep customer viscosity for its further development.
