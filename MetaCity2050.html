Through technology, the space between reality and imagination will be blurred.

Introducing Forest, a sensor specialist and simulations expert

who leverages data analysis and digital twins to form predictions about everything from world changing events to local matters. â€‹He is a part of a local digital democracy, wherein community members can raise issues to public forum.

Today, Petra raises an issue. 

In her job as a city farmer, she found a pest problem in a rooftop garden in the botanical district.

Picking up the case, Forest works with Petra to eventually arrive at two possible solutions:

Encase the garden in a glass dome or introducing a controlled bird population to eat the pests.

These solutions are put to local vote, granting residents direct control of their communities.

In this particular case, they prefer a biodiverse urban landscape and vote for introducing birds. 

Within a few weeks, the residents are happy to see the positive change implemented into their community.
