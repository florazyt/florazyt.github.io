Moth of Death

Project Overview

The client commissioned a VJ backdrop design for his new song, "Moth of Death." The desired elements included moths, flames, and the musician's logo. This design was projected as part of an art exhibition.

Creative Process

I began by researching various species of moths and their symbolic meanings, focusing on the themes of transformation and rebirth. Incorporating 3d models of moths and flames, I used dynamic animations to bring the backdrop to life. The musician's logo was seamlessly integrated into the design, ensuring it stood out while maintaining harmony with the other elements.

Achievements

  • The design received positive feedback from the audience for its vivid visuals and thematic coherence.
  • The client's art exhibition saw a 20% increase in attendance, partly attributed to the striking backdrop design.

Tools

  • TouchDesigner for creating dynamic animations and real-time visuals.
  • Adobe Illustrator for designing and integrating the musician's logo.
  • After Effects for refining and adding special effects to the animations.
