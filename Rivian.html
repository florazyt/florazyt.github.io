Rivian 2035

Type:
UI/UX, Automotive System

Duration:
09-12.2023

"In 2035, Rivian is more than just an automaker; we are the epitome of electric adventure and mobility for everyone."

We designed Rivian's cockpit in the future with the focus on community, nature-friendly and connectivity. We imagined a seamless and empowering adventure experience of technology. With a focus on the storytelling, we made a short film to demonstrate our designed features.

Rivian Community

  • Real-time updates on the latest activities of nearby Rivian drivers.

  • Members of the Rivian Community can add each other as friends and share social updates.

  • The map allows a clear view of the locations and activities of nearby social friends.

Outdoor Activities

  • Users can view various categories of outdoor activities.

  • The locations and routes on the outdoor activity map are clear, making navigation easy.

  • The activity descriptions are detailed, including local weather, estimated duration, difficulty levels, and user reviews

Animal Detection

  • 360-degree sensors can spot wild animals around and give driver a heads-up.

  • The car cameras can capture and identify wildlife.

  • Rivian updates Rivianpedia wildlife database in real time.

  • Save the moments in user’s album.
